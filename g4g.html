What are knowledge games?

Knowledge games spread knowledge by teaching messages or contributing to scientific research.

"Knowledge games produce knowledge; solve authentic, applicable problems; or generate new ideas and possibilities for real-world change"
- Karen Schrier, Knowledge Games, JHP 2016

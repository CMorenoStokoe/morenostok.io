About me


I am a human computer interaction Psychologist. I design digital services for interaction.

I am a distinguished early career researcher with a background in NHS organisation and Psychology. I have worked with organisations including Public Health Wales, NHS general practices, the University of Bristol, and I have volunteered with charity organisations including Age UK and EndCoronaVirus.org. I seek to understand how we can better design services to understand human health and deliver health care more effectively. You can read more at my researcher profile.

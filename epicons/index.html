Epicons

These are free to use icons for the display and visualisation of traits in epidemiology.

Available in EPS, PNG and SVG formats.

Psychology and behaviors

Icons suitable for traits similar to intelligence, education, socialisation, sleep, gaming, drug use, BMI, work shifts, phone use, exercise, smoking and drinking.

Wellbeing and mental health

Icons suitable for traits similar to wellbeing, depression, anxiety / worry, OCD, and loneliness.
